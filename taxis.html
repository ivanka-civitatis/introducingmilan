Taxis in Milan

Official white taxis only · Malpensa fixed €100 · Linate metered €30–€40

Taxis in Milan are safe, metered and regulated — but only if you use official vehicles. Always use official white taxis with a meter and a taxi sign on the roof. Avoid unlicensed drivers who approach you at airports, train stations or tourist areas.

✍️ By Ivanka Garcia Mancebo·Updated June 2026

How to get a taxi in Milan

Taxis can be found at designated taxi ranks near the Duomo, Centrale, Cadorna and other major spots — look for the white cars with the taxi sign. You can also book by phone or app: the main companies are RadioTaxi (02 8585) and iTaxi. Hailing a taxi in the street is possible but less common than in other cities — it is more reliable to go to a rank.

Tips to avoid being overcharged

  • Only use official white taxis with a meter, a taxi sign on the roof and a licence number displayed on the door.
  • Never accept rides from drivers who approach you unsolicited at airports or stations — these are unlicensed and may charge whatever they want.
  • The Malpensa fixed fare is €100 — if a driver quotes more, this is not legitimate.
  • Check the tariff card inside the taxi — T1 is day rate, T2 is night/Sunday/holiday. Make sure the correct tariff is shown on the meter.
  • For airport journeys, a private transfer booked in advance gives you a fixed price with no uncertainty.
⚠️ Unlicensed drivers

At Malpensa and Bergamo airports in particular, unlicensed drivers sometimes approach arriving passengers. They may dress smartly and hold signs — but they are not official taxis and are not regulated. The official taxi ranks are clearly signposted outside the arrivals exits. If in doubt, use the iTaxi app to book a verified official taxi.

Frequently asked questions

How much is a taxi in Milan?+

Taxis charge by the meter. Start fare ~€3.30 (day, 6am–9pm), ~€6.60 (night/Sunday/holidays). Malpensa to city centre: fixed €100. Linate: metered, ~€30–€40.

How do I book a taxi in Milan?+

Go to a taxi rank near the Duomo, Centrale or Cadorna, or book via the iTaxi app or phone (RadioTaxi: 02 8585). For a fixed-price airport journey, a private transfer is often more convenient.

What is the taxi fare from Malpensa Airport to Milan?+

The official fixed fare from Malpensa to central Milan is €100. No surcharges should apply. Only use official white taxis from the designated rank outside arrivals.

Are taxis in Milan safe?+

Yes — official white taxis with a meter are safe and regulated. Only use official taxis from ranks or booked via app. Avoid unlicensed drivers who approach you at airports or stations.
